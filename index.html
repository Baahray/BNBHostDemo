Getting Here

Oakbank Cottage is located in the centre of Luss village, close to Loch Lomond and local amenities.

Please note that mobile phone signal can be limited in and around the village. We recommend keeping note of relevant information.

Parking

  • Parking is available nearby within the village
  • Please park considerately and be mindful of local residents
  • Do not block driveways, access routes, or emergency access points
