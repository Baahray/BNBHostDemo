Arrival & Address

Welcome! We hope you’re really looking forward to your stay at Luss Cottage.

The cottage is tucked away in a peaceful, private spot just outside the village of Luss. The directions below are important, as standard sat navs don’t always bring you directly to the door.

Property address Oakbank Cottage Luss, Alexandria G83 8RH

What3Words location ///customers.lurching.cautious (very accurate and recommended)

Getting here

You’ll be travelling on the A82. If coming from Glasgow, drive past the green Luss Estates sign Just under 1 km later, take the first left turn There is a tall tree at the turning which makes a useful landmark

Sat Nav Tips

If using Google Maps, searching for “Luss Estates, G83 8RH” works better than the cottage name. After turning off the A82, you’ll drive onto a private road Please open and close the gate as you enter and leave You’ll see one cottage directly ahead — turn right Luss Cottage is the next cottage up the road

Parking:

You may park outside the cottage The preferred parking is in the driveway at the very end of the road The driveway gate has a latch which needs to be lifted to open Please close the gate in the evening — local deer love the flowers The driveway is narrow and on a slope, so please take care, especially when reversing.
