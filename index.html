Arrival & Address

The property address is:
Oakbank Cottage
Luss
Alexandria
G83 8RH.

You can use Google Maps to assist with your journey, however please note that Google Maps does not always pick up the exact location of the cottage.

If you are travelling from the Glasgow direction on the A82, we recommend entering “Luss Estates, G83 8RH” into Google Maps.

You will be travelling either up or down the A82. After passing the green Luss Estates sign, continue for just under one kilometre. The turning is the very first left after the sign.

There is a tall tree at the turning, which we find a helpful landmark.

(Photos of the turning and landmark will be shown here.)

After turning into the private road, you will see a gate. Please open and close this gate when entering and leaving the cottage.

(Photo of the gate will be shown here.)

As you drive through the gate, you will see a cottage directly in front of you. Turn right — Oakbank Cottage is the next cottage up.

There are only two cottages on this road, so it is easy to find.

You may park outside the cottage, however it is best to use the driveway at the very end of the road.

To access the driveway, lift the latch to open the long metal gate. Please remember to shut this gate in the evening, as deer do come in and will eat the flowers.

The driveway is tight, and care should be taken when driving up the hill, particularly if reversing.
