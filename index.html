Directions

View detailed directions

The cottage is accessed via the A82. Google Maps does not always pinpoint the exact location.

If travelling from Glasgow, enter Luss Estates, G83 8RH into Google Maps.

Continue past the green Luss Estates sign. Just under one kilometre later, take the first left. A tall tree marks the turning.

Open and close the gate on the private road when entering and leaving.

Turn right at the cottage ahead — Oakbank Cottage is the next property.

Parking

Parking is available outside the cottage. We recommend using the driveway at the end of the road.

The driveway is narrow and sloped. Please take care, particularly when reversing.

Key & Entry

The key box is located at the entrance. The code is 2016.

Check-in Time

Check-in is from 4:00 pm.
Early check-in may be possible — please message us on the day of arrival.
